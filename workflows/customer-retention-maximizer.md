### Churn Prevention Playbooks

```
Trigger: User hasn't logged in 14+ days

Auto-Actions:
Day 1: Personalized email "We miss you" with value reminder
Day 3: In-app notification with quick win suggestion
Day 7: Phone call from CSM (if high-value customer)
Day 10: Survey "What can we do better?"
Day 14: Special offer or feature unlock

Content: Always personalized based on:

- Features they were using

```

- Escalates before customer asks
