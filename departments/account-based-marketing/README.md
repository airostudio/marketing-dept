## Performance Metrics

Track these KPIs:
- **Account Engagement**: Accounts showing interest
- **Account Coverage**: % of buying committee reached
- **Pipeline from ABM**: Opportunities from target accounts
- **Deal Velocity**: Days to close for ABM accounts
- **Account Penetration**: Revenue per target account
- **Win Rate**: Close rate on ABM accounts
- **Average Deal Size**: ABM vs. non-ABM
- **Customer Lifetime Value**: ABM account value
