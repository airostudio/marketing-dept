## Performance Metrics

Track these KPIs:
- **Reach**: Total audience size across influencers
- **Engagement Rate**: Likes, comments, shares
- **EMV** (Earned Media Value): Estimated ad value
- **ROI**: Revenue / influencer marketing spend
- **CPE** (Cost Per Engagement): Spend / total engagements
- **Conversion Rate**: Sales attributed to influencers
- **Authenticity Score**: Fraud detection metrics

## Best Practices

### 1. Authenticity Over Reach
- Engagement rate > follower count
- Audience alignment matters more than size
- Look for genuine brand affinity

### 2. Clear Contracts
- Deliverables and timeline
- Usage rights
- Exclusivity clauses
- FTC disclosure requirements

### 3. Creative Freedom
- Provide guidelines, not scripts
- Trust influencer's understanding of their audience
- Review and approve, but don't micromanage

### 4. Long-term Relationships
- Ambassador programs outperform one-off posts
- Build genuine relationships
- Regular communication

### 5. Measure What Matters
- Align metrics with campaign goals
- Track beyond vanity metrics
- Attribution tracking setup

## Fraud Detection

Red flags to watch for:
- **Sudden follower spikes**: Purchased followers
- **Low engagement rate**: <2% is suspicious
- **Generic comments**: "Great post!" "Nice pic!"
- **Follower quality**: Check follower profiles
- **Engagement patterns**: Bots comment immediately

Use HypeAuditor or similar to check:
- Audience authenticity score
- Follower growth patterns
- Engagement quality
- Audience demographics

## Legal & Compliance

### FTC Requirements
- Clear disclosure of partnership
- "#ad" or "#sponsored" in content
- Disclosure must be conspicuous
- Applies to all platforms

### Contract Essentials
- Deliverables and timeline
- Compensation and payment terms
- Content usage rights
- Exclusivity and non-compete
- Performance expectations
- Termination clauses

### Rights Management
- Perpetual vs. limited usage
- Paid whitelisting rights
- Content repurposing rights
- Approval process
